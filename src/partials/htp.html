H2 How to Play

As mentioned, the game comes with 4 bonus segments;

[h2] Title

This bonus features 108 random multipliers covered by random symbols, which are shuffled before a player positions their aim and the timer starts. The player must place a bet on the position they believe the largest multiplier is hiding. The cannon will shoot the target and reveal the won multiplier when the time is up.

When selecting the wager on the 4 different multipliers, remember there are 4 bonus features you can bet on too. The coin flip bonus feature has the highest odds and chances of being selected because it's on the four spokes. There's also a multiplier on each side of the coin. Flip the coin to get the multiplier.
